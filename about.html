About Us

Muralee & Thushar, Chartered Accountants

Where Expertise Meets Accountability

Story
& History 01.

Muralee & Thushar, Chartered Accountants is a reputed firm with over 38 years of expertise in audit, assurance, taxation, and consultancy services. Founded in 1987 by CA R. Muralee Mohanan, it has grown into a trusted partnership known for integrity and excellence. Based in Adoor, the firm provides tailored financial solutions to individuals, businesses, and institutions across diverse sectors.

Our
Mission02.

To deliver reliable, value-driven, and innovative financial solutions that empower businesses and individuals to achieve sustainable growth, while upholding the highest professional standards.

Our
Vision03.

To be recognized as a leading Chartered Accountancy firm in India, known for excellence, trust, and personalized client service.
