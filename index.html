About Us

Excellence Built on Trust

Muralee & Thushar, Chartered Accountants, is a distinguished firm with over 38 years of professional experience in audit, assurance, taxation, and management consultancy services. Established in 1987 by R. Muralee Mohanan, the firm has evolved into a dynamic partnership known for its integrity, precision, and personalized client service.

Our Story

Founded in 1987 by CA R. Muralee Mohanan as a sole proprietorship, Muralee & Thushar has grown into a reputable partnership with a head office in Adoor. Over nearly four decades, we’ve built a reputation for excellence, delivering tailored financial solutions that empower individuals, businesses, and institutions.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, value-driven, and innovative financial solutions that empower businesses and individuals to achieve sustainable growth, while upholding the highest professional and ethical standards.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading Chartered Accountancy firm in India, known for excellence, trust, and dedicated client service, while continually adapting to emerging trends and client needs.
