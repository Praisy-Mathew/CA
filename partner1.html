CA R. Muralee Mohanan

Managing Partner & Founder

Professional Profile

CA R. Muralee Mohanan is a seasoned Chartered Accountant with over 38 years of experience in the field. He established the firm in 1987 as a sole proprietorship, which later evolved into Muralee & Thushar, Chartered Accountants — a respected partnership firm formed in 2023. His leadership and dedication have been pivotal in building the firm’s reputation for excellence and client trust.
