Audit & Assurance:

At Muralee & Thushar, Chartered Accountants, we provide comprehensive Audit & Assurance services, including statutory audits, internal audits, concurrent audits for banks, and specialized audit assignments aligned with industry standards and compliance requirements.

Our assurance services cover the following:

  • Statutory Audits (Including Audit of Government companies, Banks, Insurance companies, Trusts, Non-profit institutions)
  • Limited Reviews
  • Tax Audits u/s 44 AB of the Income Tax Act
  • Certifications
  • Agreed-upon procedures
  • Ind-AS Transition and Compliance reviews
  • Business Responsibility and Sustainability Reporting – Support and related Advisory
